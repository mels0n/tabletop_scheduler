### Choosing Your Love Language: Co-op vs. Competitive

When selecting a game for Valentine's Day, the most critical decision is whether your relationship thrives on **shared victory** or **friendly rivalry**.

#### The Case for Cooperation
Games like **Sky Team** or **Codenames: Duet** require couples to work toward a common goal. These are ideal for partners who want to test their empathy and communication skills without the risk of post-game resentment.

#### The Case for Competition
For couples who bond over conflict, **7 Wonders Duel** and **Splendor Duel** offer a "meaner," more strategic experience where outsmarting your partner is the primary objective. These games provide high "information gain" per session, ensuring that every move feels impactful.
