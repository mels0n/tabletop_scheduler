## Lessons Learned
- **Automation vs Cost**: We chose "Manual/Free" over "Automated/Complex". The trade-off is reliance on human discipline (mitigated by script hooks).
- **Security**: Encryption-at-rest allows for simpler storage strategies (git-ignored local files).
